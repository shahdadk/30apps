Frequently asked

Do any of these apps require an account or internet connection?

No. Every app in this collection works fully offline. There is no sign-in, no cloud sync, and no online dependency. Some apps include a link to this support page or to a privacy policy — those open in your default browser.

Will my data sync across devices?

No. Settings and saved results live on the device. There is no iCloud or third-party sync. If you uninstall the app or get a new phone, your saved state does not carry over.

Why does HikeWhistle / Morse Dot ask to use the camera?

They don't ask for camera access. They use the device's flashlight LED to strobe a visual signal, which is a separate hardware-level capability and doesn't require camera permission on iOS.

The BPM reading in TapBPM jumps around. What's wrong?

Nothing — you're seeing the meter respond to inconsistent tap intervals. Tap a bit more steadily and the confidence bar fills up. The reading averages your last eight intervals.

Why is the cocktail recipe page rated 17+?

Apple requires a 17+ rating for apps that describe alcoholic drinks, even purely educationally. Cocktail Deck is reference material — no purchase, no delivery, no targeting.

Can I request a feature?

Sure, email me. Most of these apps will stay deliberately small — the appeal of a calculator that does one thing is that it does the one thing without making you click through five screens. But I read every email.
